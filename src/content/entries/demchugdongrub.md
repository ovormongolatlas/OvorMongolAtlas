Demchugdongrub, often known as Prince De, was a descendant of Genghis Khan and a leading advocate for Inner Mongolian autonomy from Chinese Nationalist rule. He organized the Mongolian Political Council in 1933 and later headed a series of Japanese-aligned Mongol autonomous governments during the Second Sino-Japanese War.
